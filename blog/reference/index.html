The Quiet Authority of Neutral Space

January 30, 2026 · 7 min read

A neutral palette is not an absence; it is a decision. It limits options to make clarity unavoidable. In practice, it means restraint: no gradients, no decorative shadows, and a system that reads like a well-edited report.

This sample theme aims for the feeling of a government PDF at midnight — clear, calm, and built for concentration.

“International Style is less a look and more a commitment to legibility.”
